About Atelier:

Atelier as a paper posed the question: what does the crowd work equivalent of an internship look like? In order to answer this question, me and other members of Stanford’s HCI Lab designed a platform for such interactions to take place and measured the effectiveness of these internships based on how much crowd worker interns learned from their crowd worker mentors as well as the quality of their end work.
